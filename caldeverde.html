Calde Verde (Portuguese Soup)

Ingredients

  • 5 large potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 large onion, quarted
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 to 3 kale leaves, stem(s) removed
  • 1/2 a chorizo sausage (linguica works as well)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions

  1. Fill a large port with water. Add potatoes, onion, sausage, and chicken broth
  2. Cook until potatoes are soft
  3. Remove sausage
  4. Puree
  5. Add kale and cook for 15 minutes
  6. Puree
  7. Chop sausage into small pieces
  8. Add oil and sausage to pot
  9. enjoy
